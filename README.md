# 📊 Marketing Mix Analysis for Customer Acquisition

## 🔷 Overview

This project analyzes a marketing mix dataset to understand the key factors influencing **customer acquisition**. The study is based on the four Ps of marketing: **People, Product, Place, and Promotion**.

The goal is to extract actionable insights using **exploratory data analysis (EDA)** and **statistical hypothesis testing** to support data-driven marketing decisions.

---

## 🔷 Problem Statement

Marketing strategies often depend on multiple interacting factors. This project aims to:

* Understand customer demographics and behavior
* Analyze spending patterns across products
* Evaluate the effectiveness of different sales channels
* Assess the impact of marketing campaigns

---

## 🔷 Dataset Description

The dataset is structured around the four Ps of marketing:

* **People:** Birth year, education, income, etc.
* **Product:** Spending on wine, fruits, gold, etc.
* **Place:** Purchase channels (web, store, catalog)
* **Promotion:** Campaign responses and outcomes

---

* Perform **data cleaning and preprocessing**
